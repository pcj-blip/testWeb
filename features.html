Spellsy Features

Based on a simple principle—make as many words as you can from the given letters. Just you and todays seven letters. Focus on them, absorb them, find your calm… comforting and distracting in a complicated time.

Play at your own pace, no pressure. No time limit. Some puzzles are long, some are short. A new puzzle comes along every day.

No Adverts, no Data Mining

No ads, ever. You won't waste battery, cell data, or your time on ads. And we do not collect any data.

SCOWL Word List

A list of about 150,000 words curated by the dictionary already on your phone. Your choice of either UK or US English words.

Puzzles Playable Offline

Play on the tube, on an epic trip in another country, or deep in the backcountry — no cell reception required.

Statistics

For when you want them, statistics for win average, overall rank, and streaks.

Themes

Context Actions

Efficient

Small, fast, a light game that won’t take much space on your device.

Puzzle Timer
